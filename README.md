# GameZone_report

GameZone is an online retailer specializing in gaming equipment and accessories, serving customers across multiple global markets. Founded in 2019, the company sells a wide range of gaming-related products, including controllers, headsets, keyboards, and other peripherals for both casual and competitive gamers.

In recent years, GameZone has experienced steady growth as demand for gaming hardware increased worldwide. At the same time, the company faces increasing competition from large online retailers and specialized gaming stores, making data-driven decision-making increasingly important.

GameZone’s customer base has grown to over 19,000 customers, with more than 21,000 recorded transactions between 2019 and 2022, generating approximately $6.1 million in total revenue. The dataset includes information about orders, customers, products, marketing channels, purchasing platforms, and regional demand.

Reporting to the Operations team, this analysis was conducted to evaluate GameZone’s business performance over several years and identify opportunities to improve commercial results. The insights generated from this analysis are intended to support internal teams in optimizing sales performance, marketing effectiveness, and customer engagement.

The analysis focuses on the following areas:

North Star Metrics
Evaluating core performance indicators such as total revenue (GMV), number of orders, total customers, and average order value (AOV).

Sales Trends
Analyzing revenue growth and order activity over time to understand overall business performance and purchasing patterns.

Product Performance
Evaluating product demand and refund rates to identify top-performing products and potential quality issues.

Platform and Regional Insights
Examining differences between website and mobile app purchases as well as regional sales distribution to identify growth opportunities in key markets.
